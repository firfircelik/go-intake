# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant, simplified.

## Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment,
we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation
in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected behaviour

- Be welcoming. Assume good faith.
- Be concise. Stick to the technical point.
- Be respectful. Disagree with the argument, not the person.
- Be patient. People come from different backgrounds and
  experience levels.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Public or private trolling, insulting comments, or unwelcome
  attention.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit
  permission.
- Other conduct that a reasonable person would consider
  inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing
this code of conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments,
commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align
with this code of conduct.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable
behaviour may be reported to the maintainers via the same private
channel used for security reports (see `SECURITY.md`). All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
